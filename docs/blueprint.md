# **App Name**: AutoAssist Pro

## Core Features:

- Service Recommendation Tool: Suggests optimal maintenance schedules and repair services based on vehicle make, model, and user-reported issues, leveraging a reasoning tool.
- Appointment Scheduling: Allows users to book service appointments online with preferred dates and times.
- Service History: Maintains a record of past services and repairs for each vehicle.
- Interactive 3D Vehicle Models: Users can view 3D models of vehicles to better understand components and potential issues.
- FAQ and Troubleshooting: Provides a list of frequently asked questions and troubleshooting tips for common vehicle problems.
- Real-Time Quote Generation: Provides instant estimates for common repairs and services based on the selected vehicle and service type.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Navy Blue (#003366) to convey trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F4F4F4) for a clean, modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Orange (#FF7A30) to highlight key actions and CTAs.
- Headline font: 'Montserrat' (sans-serif) for a modern and bold title aesthetic.
- Body font: 'Open Sans' (sans-serif) for clear, readable text.
- Responsive design that adapts to mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.
- Use industry-standard icons for services, testimonials, and contact information, and avoid special or distracting characters that are not generally recognized.
